ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

Blue Banjo 3 (Pty) Ltd is a South African junior mining company 

Our Focus

We focus on the prospecting, development, commissioning, extraction and marketing of Coal, Copper, Platinum Group
Metals, Diamonds and other Base Metals. We secure prospecting and mining rights in and around South Africa and the Southern African region.

Compliance

We ensure compliance with all relevant legislation and regulations and pro-actively manage our risks through a structured Risk Assessment process in order to anticipate, minimise and control Occupational Health and Safety Hazards, thereby promoting a culture of “Zero Mindset”.

Principal Project

The Company's principal project is the right to prospect on a certain portion of the farm Taung 894 HA, situated in the magisterial district of Taung, North West Province, South Africa.

Embracing the Times with Our Dynamic Business Model

Blue Banjo 3 (Pty) Ltd will endeavor to generate sustainable growth in the interest of all our
stakeholders by:

  • MISSION

NEW BUSINESS

Seeking new business opportunities and partners in the minerals industry;

PARTICIPATING

Participating in the mining industry at ownership, board, management and employee
levels.

INTEGRITY

Conducting our business with integrity and complying with internationally accepted
business principles and practices.
