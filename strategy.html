BUSINESS STRATEGY

Our Business Strategy

Management’s strategy is to build the Company into a profitable mining company and
maximize shareholder value through exploration of its high quality mining properties in South
Africa and the Southern African region. The Company is committed to working with the
government and people in which it operates to achieve sustainable development goals. The
Company follows best practices in health and safety, environmental and community
engagement.

  • Strategy Requires

Our business strategy requires that we align our prospecting activities to maximize our involvement in the downstream industrial and commercial opportunities rather than simply become a supplier of raw minerals. This requires that we create value, rapidly, in the first few years of the exploration programme where drilling takes the asset from its inferred to a qualified reserve with a bankable feasibility study (BFS) and a resource asset with a defined value, and that is where we see the company’s investment interest.

Shareholder Value

Blue Banjo 3 (Pty) Ltd’s strategy is to develop substantial shareholder value through:

  • Acquiring projects where positive exploration results were achieved in the past and  these results have demonstrated the potential to be developed into mining projects;
  • Conducting sound data investigation and exploration;
  • Focused exploration and rapid evaluation of the projects; and
  • Developing its exploration projects.

Committed

We are committed to the socio-economic development of the South African government and
the communities surrounding our operations, and place sustainable development as integral
to the way we do business.

Our Objective 

Blue Banjo 3 (Pty) Ltd 's objective is to acquire, discover and define Coal, Copper, Platinum Group Metals, Diamonds and other Base Metals resources in the Southern African region. In order to realize these objectives, Blue Banjo 3 (Pty) Ltd has undertaken the following:

  • Make new discoveries by applying advanced exploration techniques over selectively  areas.
  • Acquire new properties by leveraging our strong local presence and inter company  networking within the South African mining community.
  • Complete a Feasibility Study on a certain portion of the farm Taung 894 HA.
