WELCOME TO BB3

development, commissioning, extraction and marketing of Coal, Copper, Platinum Group
Metals, Diamonds and other Base Metals. We secure prospecting and mining rights in and
around South Africa and the Southern African region.

Our Business Strategy

  • Strategy
  • Prospecting

Management’s strategy is to build the Company into a profitable mining company and maximize shareholder value through exploration of its high quality mining properties in South Africa and the Southern African region. The Company is committed to working with the government and people in which it operates to achieve sustainable development goals. The Company follows best practices in health and safety, environmental and community
engagement.

Our business strategy requires that we align our prospecting activities to maximize our involvement in the downstream industrial and commercial opportunities rather than simply become a supplier of raw minerals. This requires that we create value, rapidly, in the first few years of the exploration programme where drilling takes the asset from its inferred to a qualified reserve with a bankable feasibility study (BFS) and a resource asset with a defined
value, and that is where we see the company’s investment interest.
